Welcome, we are at your disposal to give you a fantastic holiday.

 

Rooms and ‘table d’hôtes’ in the Aude region, ideally situated in Cathar Country.

Plenty of sightseeing trips, relaxing possibilities and adventurous challenges.

Large and small pool, 10 comfortable rooms entirely renovated.

Enjoyable meals full of local flavours and regional products, partly made with home-grown vegetables from the garden and self-bred lamb.

Ingeborg, Bruno and the kids most welcome you!

With the entire crew we aim at giving you a pleasant holiday in a warm, family atmosphere.

If you have questions, we are at your disposal to give you all the information you need during a pleasant talk in our garden bar.

On top of all the peacefulness and relaxation, we can offer you the surprising aromas of our southern cuisine.

Or how would you like a cosy rustling wooden fire?

In the bar, you can consult our documentation about the region and find suggestions for numerous day trips.
